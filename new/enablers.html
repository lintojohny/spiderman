Enabler Roles & Responsibilities

Shops Onboarding
Enabler members act as Relationship Manager and simplify Shop On boarding. Enablers are enabling our managed solution concept from the get go.
Continuous Improvement
Enabler members look out for eShop's maintenance and guide shop owners on day to day operation management & guide on set work-flows.
Consultation
Enablers assist shop owners with valuable inputs on marketing campaigns, offer & promotion structuring, price correction and growth hacks.
